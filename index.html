Darrin Scott Hunter

Can you feel it?

How do you know you're having an Experience?

I design experiences across spatial, digital, and printed media. I apply the methods of fine art to lower the conscious threshold of aesthetic experience. Once visitors, users, and readers become aware that a design solution transcends the mundane, even in small ways, surprise and delight suddenly become possible. They are there—just inside a doorway, underneath a mouse click, sliding off a page edge—waiting to be discovered, an awakening, a new path, a destination yet unknown.
